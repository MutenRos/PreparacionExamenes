# Mixed Verb Tenses 3 – ANSWERS

1. He **has never had** one before.
2. There **has just been** an accident.
3. It **has been raining** for two hours and there is no sign that it will stop.
4. Do you think you **will still be doing** the job in ten years' time?
5. Most babies **learn** to speak when they are two years old.
6. When Sarah arrived at the party Bob **wasn't** there. He **had just gone** home.
7. When Ken gave up smoking two years ago, he **had been** a smoker for over 30 years.
8. I **haven't seen** Alan for ages. – The last time I **saw** him he **was looking** for a job.
9. If you need to contact me I **will be staying** at the Hilton Hotel until 5 p.m. on Friday.
10. I **haven't seen** my uncle since I came to town.
11. The train to Liverpool **leaves** at 5.46 from Platform 1.
12. Mr Taylor **is painting** the house at the moment.
13. What **do you think** about our new English teacher?
14. I **didn't study** last night and I **am not going to study** / **won't study** tonight either.
15. He usually **works** at the restaurant after school. After work he **goes** to the fitness centre twice a week.
16. She went to play tennis after she **had finished** her cheese sandwich.
17. I turned the lights on when I **heard** a strange noise.
18. We **haven't received** any news from Jack yet this week.
19. My dad **has written** many novels. At the moment he **is working** on a new spy novel.
20. It's the first time I **have ever been** to England.
