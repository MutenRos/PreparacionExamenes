# Should / Shouldn't – ANSWERS

## Exercise 1: Match statements to advice

1. I've got a headache → **B** – You shouldn't sit so close to the TV.
2. I'm cold → **E** – You should put on a jumper.
3. The game starts at three o'clock → **F** – We should leave at two-thirty.
4. He's always tired in the morning → **D** – He shouldn't stay up so late.
5. I don't feel well → **A** – You should stay in bed.
6. I want to lose weight → **C** – You should do some exercise.

## Exercise 2: Should or shouldn't

1. If it's rainy you **should** take an umbrella.
2. Tom **shouldn't** eat so many lollipops. It's bad for his teeth.
3. a) **Should** I drink hot tea if I have a sore throat?
   b) Yes, you **should**.
4. They **shouldn't** go to the cinema. They **should** stay at home and study!
5. Children **should** eat lots of vegetables but they **shouldn't** eat lots of sweets.
6. What **should** I wear? A dress or a pair of trousers?
7. You **should** eat healthy food. You **shouldn't** eat fast food. You **shouldn't** watch so much TV. You **should** walk 1 hour a day. You **should** drink fruit juice and water. You **shouldn't** drink wine or beer.
