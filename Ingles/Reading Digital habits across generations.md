## Task 1: True or False

1. More people aged 55+ use Facebook than people aged 65+. → **True** (3.5M vs 2.9M)
2. Grandparents typically use Facebook less than their grandchildren. → **False** (grandchildren are leaving the site)
3. Sheila feels grateful to social media. → **True** (she appreciates seeing grandchildren)
4. Peter found his own smartphone use affected how he felt about his children's phone use. → **True**
5. Peter has changed how much he uses his phone during the working day. → **False** (only evenings and weekends)
6. Peter feels that the changes make him a better parent. → **True** (setting a better example)
