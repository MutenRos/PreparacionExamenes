| Paragraph | Answer | Heading |
|---|---|---|

| 1 | **C** | Most young people spend too much time looking at screens. (+ D: Teens can look at more than one screen at a time) |

| 4 | **F** | Kids who use social media get more exercise than kids who play video games. |
