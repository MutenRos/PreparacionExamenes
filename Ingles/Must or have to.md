# Must or Have To – ANSWERS

## Exercise 1: Match the pairs

| Sentence | Match | Explanation |
|---|---|---|
| A – I must do my homework. | 2 – I'm telling myself it's important. | Personal obligation |
| B – I have to do my homework. | 1 – Tomorrow I have an important test. | External obligation |
| A – I must wear this new shirt for the party. | 1 – I will look smarter. | Personal choice |
| B – I have to wear a shirt and a tie to go in. | 2 – It's the rule. | External rule |
| A – I must have a drink of water. | 2 – I'm really thirsty. | Personal need |
| B – I have to drink lots of water. | 1 – The doctor told me so. | External requirement |
| A – We must go to London sometime. | 2 – It would be really nice. | Personal desire |
| B – I have to go to London. | 1 – It's another business trip. | External obligation |
| A – I must go and see the doctor. | 2 – My back hurts now and then. | Personal decision |
| B – I have to go to the doctor's. | 1 – I have an appointment. | External obligation |

## Exercise 2: Choose HAVE TO or MUST

1. I'm tired. I **must** go to bed early tonight. *(personal decision)*
2. This is a very good film. You **must** see it. *(strong recommendation)*
3. Children **have to** go to school until the age of 15. *(law/external)*
4. Mr Johnson **has to** stop smoking. The doctor said so! *(external — doctor's order)*

## Mustn't / Don't have to

1. You **mustn't** do that. *(forbidden)*
2. You **don't have to** ask my permission. *(no obligation)*
3. You **mustn't** speak to the driver. *(dangerous)*
4. You **don't have to** ask. *(not necessary)*
5. You **mustn't** park here. *(forbidden)*
6. You **don't have to** do it immediately. *(no urgency)*
7. We **don't have** to go if you don't want to. *(optional)*
8. You **mustn't** tell anybody else. *(secret)*
9. You **mustn't** click with the right mouse button. *(forbidden)*
10. You **mustn't** be late. *(forbidden)*
11. He **doesn't have to** work. *(no obligation)*
12. I **don't have to** go to work. *(no obligation)*
13. You **mustn't** forget what I said. *(forbidden)*
14. You **don't have to** wear a tie. *(optional)*
15. You **don't have to** change trains. *(not necessary)*
16. You **mustn't** hit your opponent below the belt. *(forbidden)*
17. I **don't have to** wear a suit on Fridays. *(not required)*
18. You **mustn't** start before the gun is fired. *(forbidden)*
19. You **mustn't** look at other people's cards. *(forbidden)*
20. You **don't have to** be mad to work here. *(not necessary)*
