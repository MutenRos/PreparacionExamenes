# General Vocabulary – ANSWERS

Complete the sentences (two words not needed: **kindly**, **thorough** are the unused ones — see note):

1. The population growth in the city was incredibly **rapid**.
2. The laws in different countries **vary** greatly.
3. Pollution is a **global** problem.
4. Everyone **combined** their efforts and defeated the invader.
5. The artist uses **subtle** differences in colour in her landscapes.
6. The perfect **beverage** for a five-course meal is water.
7. An **immense** amount of money was needed to buy such a large boat.
8. The student was **conscious** of the others staring at her.
9. Alex **disapproved** of her son's behaviour.
10. Barking is **typical** behaviour of dogs.
11. The huge military plane was so big. It was an **awesome** sight.
12. The first chocolate they produced was very different from **contemporary** chocolate.
13. Constant training and planning for the event led to **eventual** success.
14. He did a **thorough** job of cleaning the stains out of the carpet.

> Nota: Las dos palabras sobrantes son **kindly** y **evaluate**.
