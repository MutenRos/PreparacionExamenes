## Short Answer Questions

**1. How do modern technologies impact students' interaction in educational settings?**
Modern technologies have changed how students learn and interact. Students now use tablets and laptops to access learning materials easily, find information quickly, make notes during lessons, and engage more actively in class discussions and activities.

**2. What is one benefit of online learning platforms mentioned in the text?**
Online learning platforms allow students to study at their own pace and manage their schedules better. They can access video lectures, assignments, and quizzes from anywhere with internet access, which helps learners balance study with other responsibilities such as work or family.

**3. What is the function of interactive whiteboards in classrooms according to the text?**
Interactive whiteboards are used to make learning more dynamic. Teachers can display images, videos, or interactive diagrams to explain difficult concepts, making lessons more engaging and helping students understand better than with traditional methods. Students can also interact with the content.

**4. How do mobile apps contribute to education, as discussed in the text?**
Mobile apps offer new ways to learn languages or develop skills. Users can practice vocabulary, grammar, or problem-solving exercises on their phones whenever they have free time. Many apps provide instant feedback, allowing learners to self-assess and improve over time, which motivates students to keep practicing regularly.
